Understanding the risk process

Understanding the lifecycle of a risk will help you become a more effecient and effective risk manager.

Risks come in many different shapes and sizes, our risk process is designed to support teams and help them manage thier own risks effectively.

Before you start: have you done any risk management training.

  • Identify a risk

    Knowing how to identify risk is a critical first step in risk management. Use our online resources to help you identify a risk.

    Get help to identify a risk.

  • Assess a risk

    Understanding how a risk can impact a project is vital. Use our online calculator to help you with a risk assessment.

    Use our calculator and get help to assess a risk.

  • Create an action plan

    Define any actions that will help reduce or remove the risk.

    Get help to reduce the impact of a risk.

  • Record a risk

    Access the risk management system (CRIS) to record your risk.

    Record a risk on the risk management system.

  • Monitor a risk

    When you have recorded a risk, consider how often you monitor the risk and steps you can take towards reducing its impact.

    Get help to monitor a risk.

  • Close a risk

    Once you have dealt with a risk you will need to close it.

    Get help to close a risk.
