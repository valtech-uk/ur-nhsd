Scoring risk

Without a way to score risk, teams would continually struggle to communicate about how to allocate resources appropriately in order to minimize costs and impact to our business.

Risk at NHS Digital is scored on a scale from 1 to 25. 1 is the lowest risk score, 25 is the highest risk score.
