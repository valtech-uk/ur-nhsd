What is corporate performance and analysis?

Corporate performance and analysis is a team of people who are responsible for the health of NHS Digital as an organisation. This is measured in many different ways from workforce diversity to financial performance.

How can we help you?

Our group offer a range of products and services to help you measure something business related. This is known as a performance indicator.

Help with performance indicator creation

We can help you understand what parts of your product, service or team you should measure as well as give you support in managing your performance indicator.

View all current performance indicators.

Help with performance indicator reviews

We can also help to review your performance indicator to make sure it is still valid and still providing what you need it to. Reviews generally take 2 days and you will need to book in with our team.

Book a performance indicator review.
